Introducing Nick Song

My name is Nick Song. I'm a multimedia journalist with a specialty in audio and radio. While I'm originally from Los Angeles, I am currently interested in reporting on local and business stories within New York City.

List of my favorite journalism stories:

  • The Falling Man by Tom Junod

    This article delves into the identity of an unidentified man photographed jumping from the World Trade Center during 9/11. Junod uses care and tact to explore the topic, making the piece memorable to all those who read it.

  • The Problem We All Live With by This American Life

    I enjoyed how Nikole Hannah Jones explores a school district's implementation of a desegregation program in their schools. The piece placed the seemingly historic notion of desegregation in the present and made it relevant to the listener.

  • Hiroshima by John Hersey

    The New Yorker dedicated an entire issue to Hersey's when it was released in 1946. Hersey describes the violent reality of an atomic bombing in plain English for readers just one year after the attacks on Hiroshima and Nagasaki.
